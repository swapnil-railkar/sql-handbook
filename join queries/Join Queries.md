#### Introduction : 

Join queries are used to get data from more than one table by joining them. This tables are usually
get joined by [[FOREIGN KEY]]s.

#### Types of joins : 

1. [[LEFT JOIN]] (Left outer join)
2. [[RIGHT JOIN]] (Right outer join)
3. [[INNER JOIN]]
4. [[FULL JOIN]]
5. [[CROSS JOIN]]
6. [[SELF JOIN]]
7. [[NATURAL JOIN]]
